Meet Silkthemes!
Silkthemes is a community project that aims to be the best place for you to share and install themes for Zen Browser.

Silkthemes is still in heavy development. There may be bugs and missing features, use at your own risk.
How it works
Silkthemes comes in two parts: the theme store and the frontend.

The theme store is where most of the improvements we've made to Zen Mods are. It features userContent support, easier submission/update workflows, significantly improved vetting process, clearer submission guidelines, and more.

Meanwhile, the frontend allows you to install and manage themes from the store, kind of like how you do it with Zen Mods.
